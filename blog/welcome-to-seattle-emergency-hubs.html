Welcome to Seattle Emergency Hubs

March 1, 2026 · Seattle Emergency Hubs Team

We're thrilled to launch the new Seattle Emergency Hubs website! This site is your central hub for information about our organization, our neighborhood hubs, upcoming events, and preparedness resources.

Our Mission

Seattle Emergency Hubs is a grassroots organization dedicated to emergency preparedness and mutual aid across Seattle's neighborhoods. We believe that resilient communities start at the neighborhood level — with neighbors who know each other, have basic supplies, and have a plan.

Our work is built on three pillars:

  1. Community Hubs: Neighborhood gathering points staffed by trained volunteers, ready to coordinate mutual aid and emergency response after a disaster.
  2. Education: Teaching households the basics of emergency preparedness, from 72-hour kits to communication plans.
  3. Mutual Aid: Building the neighbor-to-neighbor networks that allow communities to support one another when formal services are stretched.

What You'll Find on This Site

  • Blog: Updates, preparedness tips, event recaps, and stories from our hubs.
  • Calendar: Upcoming events, trainings, and hub meetings.
  • Neighborlink Map: An interactive map showing all active and forming hubs across Seattle.
  • Hubs: Individual pages for each neighborhood hub with contact info, meeting details, and volunteer opportunities.
  • Resources: Educational guides, promotional posters, training manuals, and external links.

Get Involved

The best thing you can do is find your local hub and get involved. If there isn't a hub in your neighborhood yet, contact us — we'll help you start one.

You can also attend an upcoming event, download our preparedness resources, or join our mailing list to stay informed.

Thank you for being part of building a more resilient Seattle.

— The Seattle Emergency Hubs Team
