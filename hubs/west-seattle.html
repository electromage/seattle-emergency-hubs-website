About This Hub

The West Seattle Hub serves the communities west of the Duwamish River, including the Alaska Junction area, the Admiral District, and South Park. Founded in 2022, it was one of our earliest hubs and has developed a strong volunteer culture.

The hub has established close relationships with the West Seattle Fire Station and coordinates regularly with the Seattle OEM (Office of Emergency Management) district coordinator for West Seattle and South Park.

What We Do

  • Maintain emergency supply caches for neighborhood distribution
  • Train volunteers in first aid, CERT, and disaster response
  • Facilitate neighbor-to-neighbor mutual aid networks
  • Host regular community preparedness meetings and events
  • Coordinate with Seattle OEM and local fire stations

Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned CERT volunteer or just getting started, there's a place for you at the West Seattle Hub. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels.
