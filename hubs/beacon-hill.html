About This Hub

The Beacon Hill Hub has been active since 2022, when a group of neighbors decided to take community preparedness into their own hands. Today it is one of the most established hubs in our network, with trained volunteers spread across the neighborhood.

The hub maintains two supply caches — one at the library and one at Jefferson Park — stocked with first-aid supplies, water filtration equipment, and communication tools.

What We Do

  • Maintain emergency supply caches for neighborhood distribution
  • Train volunteers in first aid, CERT, and disaster response
  • Facilitate neighbor-to-neighbor mutual aid networks
  • Host regular community preparedness meetings and events
  • Coordinate with Seattle OEM and local fire stations

Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned CERT volunteer or just getting started, there's a place for you at the Beacon Hill Hub. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels.
