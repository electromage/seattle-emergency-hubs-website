About This Hub

The Rainier Valley Hub is currently in formation. A dedicated group of seven neighbors is working to establish a hub for this important and diverse part of Seattle. We are actively looking for volunteers, especially those with CERT or first-aid experience, to help lead this effort.

Rainier Valley is a high-priority area for our network due to its size and the number of residents who would benefit from a local hub. If you live in Columbia City, Rainier Beach, or South Beacon Hill, we need your help!

Join the founding team! If you can commit a few hours a month, contact us to become a founding volunteer for the Rainier Valley Hub.

What We Do

  • Maintain emergency supply caches for neighborhood distribution
  • Train volunteers in first aid, CERT, and disaster response
  • Facilitate neighbor-to-neighbor mutual aid networks
  • Host regular community preparedness meetings and events
  • Coordinate with Seattle OEM and local fire stations

Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned CERT volunteer or just getting started, there's a place for you at the Rainier Valley Hub. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels.
