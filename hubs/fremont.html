About This Hub

The Fremont Hub serves the north-central part of Seattle, including the quirky Fremont neighborhood and neighboring Wallingford and Green Lake communities. Founded in 2023, it has quickly grown to become a well-organized hub with a strong volunteer base.

Known for its creative outreach, the Fremont Hub regularly participates in community events and fairs to spread the word about emergency preparedness.

What We Do

  • Maintain emergency supply caches for neighborhood distribution
  • Train volunteers in first aid, CERT, and disaster response
  • Facilitate neighbor-to-neighbor mutual aid networks
  • Host regular community preparedness meetings and events
  • Coordinate with Seattle OEM and local fire stations

Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned CERT volunteer or just getting started, there's a place for you at the Fremont Hub. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels.
