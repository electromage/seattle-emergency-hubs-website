About This Hub

The Capitol Hill Hub is our largest hub by volunteer count, reflecting the dense, engaged community in this vibrant neighborhood. It was founded in 2021 and has since grown to cover Capitol Hill, First Hill, and parts of the Central District.

The hub partners closely with local community organizations and hosts one of our most active training programs, including CERT certification courses three times per year.

What We Do

  • Maintain emergency supply caches for neighborhood distribution
  • Train volunteers in first aid, CERT, and disaster response
  • Facilitate neighbor-to-neighbor mutual aid networks
  • Host regular community preparedness meetings and events
  • Coordinate with Seattle OEM and local fire stations

Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned CERT volunteer or just getting started, there's a place for you at the Capitol Hill Hub. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels.
